The Arrival

15 April 2023 — General

Today I received my Yamaha MT-25 from Puncak Alam Motoworld Sdn Bhd.

I decided a few months ago to ride to work hence I am on the quest to find a suitable motorcycle that can fulfill my needs. After reading and watching various reviews of all the targeted models, I finally decided to get Yamaha MT-25 which is the highest number of CC that my Malaysian Driving License allows me to ride.

I am excited yet I am nervous to ride since I have not ride any bike for the past 20 years. I am practically on the edge when I mount the bike for the very first time but I managed to claim my nerves.

Before I have my bike with me, I glanced through the User Manual so that I have some idea of how to operate the machine. Since I am out of the motorcycle scene for a long time, there are many new features I am not familiar with.
