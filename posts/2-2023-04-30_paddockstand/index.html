Paddock Stand

30 April 2023 — Tools

If you’re a motorcycle enthusiast, you know how important it is to have a good paddock stand. It’s an essential tool for working on your bike, whether you’re changing the oil, adjusting the chain, or doing any other maintenance task. But what if you don’t have a paddock stand? What if you’re stuck without one, and you need to work on your bike?

Without the rear paddock stand, I am having some difficulties performing all those tasks hence, I decided to get myself the rear paddock stand while I was at Serdang Motorcycle yesterday.

Product Description

  1. Rear paddock stand for all motorcycles up to 250kg
  2. Get both U-Shape and L-Shape Bracket Hook Holders (switchable)
  3. Usable on bobbins (U-Shape) and swingarms (L-Shape)
  4. Easy to assemble and an instruction guide is provided
  5. Lift your rear wheel effortlessly for repair, maintenance, and storage

Installation of the rear paddock stand only took me approximately 15 minutes to set up with all the tools in my toolbox.

My Comments

Here’s my review of the paddock stand:-

  • Easy installation as the guide shows clear instructions.
  • Sturdy iron as it does not wobble when I have it on my bike.
  • The product comes with some metal shavings, hence need to get rid of it before installation.
